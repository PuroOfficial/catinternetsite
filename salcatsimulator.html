SalCatSimulator recommends Cat Internet!

Cat Internet is a Community App designed to Revive Cat Simulator, Make discussions, share screenshots of your score or flex your avatar that you made in Cat Simulator, or selfie with a player you met in multiplayer, It's up to you!


Welcome to Cat Internet, Where Cat Simulator 2015 community comes to hang out, flex their currency, share funny screenshots, give guides to other people!

What can you do in Cat Internet?:

You can share you're progress and get feedback on what to do and how to grind, Share you're community artwork, Share funny screenshots with the community to get a good laughs or just to celebrate a meet-up!
